2023 Toyota Camry

1. LE

Starting MSRP: $26,220

The LE is the base model, but it’s loaded with features that offer a great combination of style, safety, and technology, perfect for everyday drivers looking for comfort and reliability.

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 203 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 39 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: LED headlights with automatic on/off, 17-inch alloy wheels
  • Interior Comfort: Fabric-trimmed seats, power-adjustable driver’s seat
  • Technology: 7-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Amazon Alexa
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with Pre-Collision System, Lane Departure Alert, Adaptive Cruise Control

Ideal for: Buyers who want a high-quality, budget-friendly option with modern conveniences.

2. SE

Starting MSRP: $27,760

The SE adds a sporty edge to the Camry with enhanced styling and a sport-tuned suspension.

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 203 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 39 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 18-inch black alloy wheels, black sport mesh grille, rear spoiler
  • Interior Comfort: SofTex-trimmed seats with contrast stitching, leather-trimmed steering wheel with paddle shifters
  • Technology: Enhanced audio and upgraded infotainment
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with advanced safety features

Ideal for: Those looking for sporty design and a dynamic driving experience without breaking the bank.

3. SE Nightshade Edition

Starting MSRP: $28,760

The SE Nightshade Edition takes the SE trim to the next level with distinctive blacked-out elements for a sleek, sophisticated look.

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 203 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 39 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 19-inch black alloy wheels, black window trim, and black badging
  • Interior Comfort: SofTex-trimmed seats
  • Technology: Standard infotainment with additional styling cues
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with driver assistance features

Ideal for: Drivers who want a sportier look with a sleek, all-black exterior theme.

4. XLE

Starting MSRP: $30,970

The XLE adds a layer of luxury to the Camry lineup, with premium materials and advanced technology for those who want a bit more refinement.

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 203 horsepower (optional 3.5L V6)
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 39 MPG (4-cylinder) on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 18-inch chrome alloy wheels, LED headlights and taillights
  • Interior Comfort: Leather-trimmed heated front seats, 8-way power-adjustable driver’s seat
  • Technology: 9-inch touchscreen, 7-inch Multi-Information Display, wireless charging
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with blind-spot monitor and rear cross-traffic alert

Ideal for: Drivers looking for a luxury feel and more comfort-oriented features.

5. XSE

Starting MSRP: $31,520

The XSE combines the luxurious elements of the XLE with a sporty design, offering a mix of high-end features and performance-inspired aesthetics.

  • Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 206 horsepower (optional 3.5L V6)
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 38 MPG (4-cylinder) on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 19-inch gloss-black alloy wheels, sport mesh grille, dual exhaust
  • Interior Comfort: Leather-trimmed sport seats, optional ventilated front seats
  • Technology: 9-inch infotainment, JBL premium audio option
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with front and rear parking assist

Ideal for: Those seeking a balance of sporty design, luxury, and technology.

6. TRD

Starting MSRP: $33,285

The TRD model is built for performance enthusiasts, offering the most athletic drive with unique styling and a more aggressive stance.

  • Engine: 3.5L V6 engine with 301 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 31 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 19-inch matte black alloy wheels, aerodynamic body kit, blacked-out badging
  • Interior Comfort: Sport SofTex-trimmed seats with red stitching, TRD floor mats
  • Technology: 9-inch touchscreen infotainment, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, Alexa compatibility
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with enhanced stability control

Ideal for: Drivers who want a performance-oriented Camry with exclusive TRD styling and handling upgrades.

7. XLE V6

Starting MSRP: $36,395

The XLE V6 combines luxury and powerful performance, featuring the 3.5L V6 engine and a range of premium amenities.

  • Engine: 3.5L V6 engine with 301 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 33 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 18-inch alloy wheels, dual chrome exhaust, LED taillights
  • Interior Comfort: Premium leather-trimmed seats, optional heated and ventilated front seats
  • Technology: 9-inch touchscreen display, JBL premium audio, Head-Up Display option
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with Intelligent Clearance Sonar

Ideal for: Buyers who want an upscale Camry with V6 power and advanced luxury options.

8. XSE V6

Starting MSRP: $36,895

The XSE V6 brings together sporty performance with luxurious upgrades, making it the top-of-the-line choice for those who want the best of both worlds.

  • Engine: 3.5L V6 engine with 301 horsepower
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 32 MPG on the highway
  • Exterior Features: 19-inch gloss-black wheels, quad chrome-tipped exhaust, optional panoramic roof
  • Interior Comfort: Leather-trimmed sport seats, heated and ventilated front seats, ambient lighting
  • Technology: 9-inch infotainment, JBL premium audio, wireless charging, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense 2.5+ with rear cross-traffic alert, parking assist with automatic braking

Ideal for: Drivers who want a high-performance Camry with luxury features and a sporty appearance.
